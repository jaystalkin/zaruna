About

Zaruna is a genre-bending alt-rock trio from the West Midlands, UK, known for their gritty 2000s aesthetic and unpredictable, high-energy sound. Blending the fuzzed-out punch of Foo Fighters, the reggae-laced grooves of The Police, and the experimental spirit of Ween, Zaruna delivers a sound that's raw, DIY, and proudly weird.

From heavy riffs to off-kilter rhythms, soft ballads to reggae-infused jams, Zaruna shifts effortlessly between styles—making each track a surprise and every live show unforgettable. The band plays every gig like it’s their last, channeling their passion into performances that linger long after the final note.

With a love for the greats and a dedication to carving their own sonic path, Zaruna appeals to a wide, mixed audience—there’s truly something for everyone. Catch their latest musical experiments on all streaming platforms.
